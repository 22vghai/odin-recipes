Tacos

Description

These chicken tacos seasoned with lime, oregano, and garlic are flavorful and delicious. I found this recipe on the internet late one night, and it has become of my favorite dinner meals!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast meat - cubed
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • ⅛ cup red wine vinegar
  • ½ lime, juiced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 10 (6 inch) flour tortillas
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • ¼ cup shredded lettuce
  • ¼ cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • ¼ cup salsa

Steps

  1. Sauté chicken in a medium saucepan over medium high heat until tender, about 15 to 20 minutes. Add green onions, vinegar, lime juice, garlic, oregano, sugar, salt, and pepper. Simmer over low heat for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat an iron skillet over medium heat. Place a tortilla in the pan, warm, and turn over to heat the other side. Repeat with remaining tortillas. Serve lime chicken mixture in warm tortillas topped with tomato, lettuce, cheese and salsa.
